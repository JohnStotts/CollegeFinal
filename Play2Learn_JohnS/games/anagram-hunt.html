Anagram Scramble

Anagrams are words formed by rearranging the letters of another word, such as 'cinema' can be formed from 'iceman'.
  • Select the word length and press the 'Start' button.
  • A word will appear with the number of anagrams it has.
  • Include that word and all you can think of!
  • Hurry! You have 30s per round.
